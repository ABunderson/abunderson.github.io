Why we are here.

The Highland-Chamber of commerce is here to help local businesses grow. We want everyone to be able to fulfill their dreams of being an entrepenuer and want to support them on their way. We are a place where different businesses can gather and learn from each other. We are a place where the little people stand together and become strong. We are commited to helping every business thrive.

Business Highlights

The Painted Daisy Florist

This locally owned flowershop is set up to meet the needs of all. They won best business in the Chamber fall of 2019. No order is too small or too big for these talented floral designers to handle.

Timpanogos Smiles Dentistry

This dentistry prides itself on meeting the needs of all their clients no matter what age. They are kid friendly and great with adults. They also handle emergency visits!

The Makers Club

This local club had a small start. From working in a garage to working in their current shop. They have always been happy to help. This is the place to go to learn how to make all those cool projects you always wished you could craft. Sign up today for one of their classes!
