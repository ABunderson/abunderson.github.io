Which is better?

A question as old as time. Which weather is better? One side of the argument includes curling up with a book and some hot cocoa in front of a roaring fire while a frigid storm reigns outside. The other side has hot summer days full of exploration of the great outdoors. Is winter better than summer or vice versa? If the world was going to end in fire or ice which would you prefer?
