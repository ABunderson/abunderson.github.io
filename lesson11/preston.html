Local Wildlife

By: Adrienne Bunderson

Preston, Idaho has a variety of wildlife that can easily be found if one only looks. There are a lot of birds, reptiles, and mammals. This is possible because of the great bounty and beauty of the land around Preston, Idaho. It is important to respect wildlife when exploring our amazing wilderness. Animals can be greatly affected by the actions of humans that visit their territories. One small piece of trash discarded in the wrong area can cost an innocent creature its life. All ecosystems have a delicate balance. If one species is negatively affected all the rest of the animals may suffer.

We welcome you to visit our beautiful town, but we ask you to be respectful of those that were there before you and those that will have to live or die with the consequences of your actions after you leave.
