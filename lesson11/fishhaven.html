Best Fish?

By: Adrienne Bunderson

Are you a fishing fanatic? If so, Fish Haven is the town you need to visit. It is right next to the greatly stocked Bear Lake. For those who do not know anything about fishing (like me) this is a great place to learn and have a great time trying. It is enjoyable even when you do not catch any fish. There are big fish and small fish and there may even be catfish if you are determined enough to look. There is fishing on docks and fishing on boats. You can fly fish, you can fish with worms, and you can fish with lures. Any type of fishing is up for grabs. If you want to try catching a fish with your hands, I will bring a camera to record it for your prosperity.
However, you want to fish, Fish Haven is the place to be. Bring a camera and make memories that will last forever. Much longer than any fish would last in the fridge.
